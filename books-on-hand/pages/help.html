Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Buying Books

How do I purchase a book?

Simply browse our collection, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. We accept various payment methods including credit cards and PayPal.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for books in their original condition. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

Selling Books

How do I list a book for sale?

Go to the Sell page, fill out the book details form, upload photos, and set your price. Your listing will be reviewed and published within 24 hours.

What are the seller fees?

We charge a 10% commission on successful sales. There are no upfront listing fees.

Account & Security

How do I create an account?

Click the user icon in the top right corner and select "Sign Up". Fill in your details to create your account.

How secure is my information?

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and payment information. Your data is never shared with third parties.
