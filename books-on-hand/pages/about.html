About Books On Hand

Connecting book lovers with their next great read

Our Mission

At Books On Hand (BOH), we believe that every book deserves a second chance and every reader deserves access to affordable literature. Our mission is to create a sustainable marketplace where book lovers can buy and sell pre-loved books, making reading more accessible and environmentally friendly.

Our Values

Accessibility

Making books accessible to everyone through competitive pricing

Sustainability

Promoting the reuse of books to reduce environmental impact

Community

Building a community of book lovers who share and discuss

Trust

Ensuring safe and secure transactions for all users

Our Team

Books On Hand is powered by a passionate team of book lovers, technology experts, and customer service professionals who work together to create the best possible experience for our community.
